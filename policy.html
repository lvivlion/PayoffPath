Privacy & Permissions Transparency

We believe in full transparency regarding how PayoffPath interacts with your browser.

  • Storage Permission PayoffPath saves your loan settings (such as original balance, interest rate, and payment history) and user preferences locally on your device. This ensures your data persists across sessions so you don’t have to manually re-enter your details every time you visit your lender’s dashboard.
  • ActiveTab Permission We use a privacy-first approach. This permission grants the extension temporary access to your focused tab only when you interact with it. This allows PayoffPath to scan for loan balances and interest rates solely upon your explicit request.
  • Scripting Permission This is required to programmatically inject the PayoffPath simulation dashboard directly into the lender’s webpage. It allows us to provide a premium, integrated overlay that calculates interest savings in real-time without requiring you to switch tabs.
  • Host Permission Since loans are hosted on a vast array of domains—from large national banks to local credit unions—broad host access is necessary. This allows the extension to identify and scan for relevant financial data on whichever secure lender portal you are currently using.
